CASE STUDY 2: Staying ahead of your competitors by addressing your distribution network in 8 cities in 3 weeks

For almost 10 years we produced roadshows for BT Funds Management in Australia. The audience was their sales and distribution partners – the channel to 80% of the investors in their retail funds.

In addition to arranging design and logistics over many months, we wrote and produced videos and worked with all the plenary and breakout presenters to refine content and rehearse delivery.

CASE STUDY 3: A three day client retreat for a company’s most influential and wealthy clients

We were responsible for the creative and strategic direction and execution of an event for 40 Private Wealth Management clients from all around Asia, as well as China’s wealthiest man and the division’s most senior executives.

The event was held in an exclusive and remote resort in China, where clients were offered investment seminars, golf tournaments, golf pro tuition, a high-end fashion show, a circus acrobat show, fine dining dinners, casual elegant lunches, private city tours, and a trip to Shanghai for a Berlin Philharmonic Orchestra performance and private drinks with performers in an exclusive hotel.

All aspects of the event ran like clockwork and the personal, professional attention to detail helped enhance relationships between the bank and its clients.
