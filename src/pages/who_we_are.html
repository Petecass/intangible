Peter Browne

“We all communicate with great passion and enthusiasm – the trick is to be able to do it to our audience as easily as to our family and friends.”

Peter trained as an actor for 3 years at NIDA in Sydney, Australia and as a film-maker at Swinburne Institute of Technology in Melbourne, Australia. He has worked as a professional actor since 1981.

From 1992 he worked on corporate communication projects, writing and producing conferences, events, presentations and videos. On these projects, he has worked with hundreds of executives – from graduates to CEOs – guiding them to tell their story clearly and with impact.

And since 2014 he has focused on delivering formal coaching and training sessions, combining his entertainment and corporate experience to develop confident, passionate presenters.

Jacqui Browne

“The first place to start is with your client. Know your client - understand what they want, what drives them and deliver that. Then you have the basis of a great relationship. ”

For over 15 years, Jacqui worked as a marketing executive in the financial services sector – initially with BT Funds Management, and then Deutsche Bank Private Wealth Management in Sydney. From 2003 she became the Asia-Pacific Head of Marketing for Deutsche Bank Private Wealth Management, based in Singapore.

Over this time, she was responsible for the development and execution of the business marketing strategies across the region. This has included the delivery of high-end corporate and social events for Deutsche Bank Private Wealth Management’s wealthiest clients and senior executives – spanning from boardroom meetings and ballroom investor presentations to exclusive sporting, arts and cultural events across Asia-Pacific and Australia.

Since 2010 she has worked with Intangible Communications’ clients on events, marketing, copywriting and video production.
