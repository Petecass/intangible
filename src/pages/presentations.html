We meet your needs with customised tailored programs

We offer coaching and training in four vital areas that can be separated or combined.

Get your message right for all occassions

“Complexity is your enemy. Any fool can make something complicated. It is hard to keep things simple.” Richard Branson

Crafting your content is a challenging exercise. One of the dangers is that our key messages get swamped in detail and we lose our audience.

Applying communication principles refined over centuries by dramatic writers, and more recently journalists, gives you a framework to create a compelling narrative for your content.

Master your body language, vocal dynamics, and content delivery

“There are always three speeches, for every one you actually gave. The one you practiced, the one you gave, and the one you wish you gave.” Dale Carnegie

What do we do with our body and voice to communicate with integrity? More importantly, what are we doing – and is our body and voice aligned with our content or sending a different message altogether?

Exercising presentation techniques and skills gives you the confidence to perform in the same way that elite athletes benefit from coaching and training.

Speak to be understood

“Speak clearly, if you speak at all. Carve every word before you let it fall.” Oliver Wendell Holmes

We all speak the English language differently – and sometimes our audiences have difficulty understanding what we are saying.

This is an area which can be controlled so our audience understands every word we say and our messages are clearly received.

We explain the mechanics of articulation and correct pronunciation and through a series of exercises guide you to improve your vocal clarity to a professional and

Prepare for a pitch or event

“It usually takes me more than three weeks to prepare a good impromptu speech.” Mark Twain

Allocating time to prepare for a critical presentation is straightforward – but having the right process to get ‘pitch perfect’ in the shortest possible time can be challenging.

We help you refine your story and then rehearse and practise so you are confident, relaxed and ready to inspire the crowd.
